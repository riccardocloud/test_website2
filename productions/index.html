Short Film

Mr. Smith

Mr. Smith could earnestly claim to have seen it all. But he knew that further adventures await, and so he came to Maastricht. The Speakeasy, located in a cellar in Wyck, is at once exclusive and inviting. It embodies the qualities he values most highly: intrigue, discretion and above all, a good story over a strong drink.

The bartenders strive to convey this story for each guest through finely and thoughtfully crafted cocktails. Each drink evokes an episode of Mr. Smith's enchanted and globetrotting life.

Major Collaborative Projects

Royals Cup Aftermovie 2022

Over three thrilling days, witness the fierce competition and vibrant festivities that define this traditional rivalry among Europe's top universities. Shot alongside Loffe and Tim, the event is immortalized in an action-packed aftermovie, followed by a pristine music mashup by Luca Peroni.

Los Sueños De Los Muertos

An award-winning short film shot in a Western-style rendition of Maastricht. From camera operation to drone work, every scene was an opportunity to blend storytelling with kinetic visuals while collaborating with Christopher Jorna and Victor Moroianu.
